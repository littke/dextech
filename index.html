About us

DexTech develops drug candidates for urologic oncology, especially prostate cancer, based on glycosylation engineering.

Close connection between R&D and clinical oncology ensures development of tools for real clinical problems.

DexTech was established in 2004 by:

  • Anders R Holmberg, a chemical engineer with a PhD in experimental oncology. He is an expert in glycosylation chemistry.
  • Sten Nilsson (MD, PhD), a specialist in clinical oncology (urologic oncology, especially prostate cancer).

Anders R Holmberg is managing director and Sten Nilsson is our clinical expert and member of the board.
